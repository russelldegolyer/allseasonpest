Core Service

General Pest Control

Our general pest service is built for common pest's around the home and is designed to help keep pest activity.

This service typically targets pests such as ants, spiders, crickets, earwigs, silverfish, centipedes, millipedes, and other common invading insects. Does not cover bed bugs or termites.

Typical Coverage

Exterior foundation and eave treatment with full front to back curb spot treatment as needed based on pest activity, pressure, and customer concerns.

Important Note

Interior and garage treatments are optional, not mandatory.

Monitoring + Control

Rodent Control

Our rodent control service focuses on reducing and monitoring rodent activity through strategic bait placement and ongoing follow-up where needed.

Service includes inspection of active areas, exterior bait stations, sticky traps, and recommendations to help reduce conditions that contribute to rodent pressure around the home.

Service Focus

Ongoing monitoring, activity reduction, baiting, and follow-up based on the level of rodent pressure.

Important Note

Does not include moles, snakes, skunks, or squirrels.

Targeted Outdoor Service

Tick & Flea Treatment

Tick and flea treatments are designed for properties dealing with biting pests in the yard, around pet areas, or in outdoor spaces where activity tends to build up.

These treatments focus on helping reduce active populations and improving control in the areas where ticks and fleas are most likely to be found.

Best For

Homes with pets, shaded yards, wildlife traffic, or recurring tick and flea activity outside.

Important Note

This is an optional add-on service for general pest control.

Seasonal Treatment

Mosquito Treatment

Our mosquito treatments are designed to help reduce mosquito pressure in the yard so you can enjoy outdoor areas more comfortably during the warmer months.

Treatment is typically focused on shaded foliage, harborage areas, and other outdoor zones where mosquitoes like to rest and build up.

Best For

Homes with patios, backyards, pets, outdoor gatherings, or heavy mosquito pressure during the season.

Service Approach

Monthly recurring treatments from April-September aimed at reducing biting pressure and breeding populations.

Specialized Treatment

German Roach Treatment

German roach problems usually require a more aggressive and targeted treatment plan than standard pest control. This service is built for active infestations that need more focused attention.

Treatment may include detailed inspection, targeted crack-and-crevice applications, baiting, and follow-up service depending on the level of activity and conditions inside the home.

Best For

Active german roach infestations that need more than routine recurring service.

Important Note

Success depends heavily on severity, sanitation, clutter levels, and cooperation with preparation recommendations.

Specialized Treatment

Weed Control

Our weed control service is designed to help reduce unwanted weed growth and improve the overall appearance of your property.

Treatment plans may vary based on the season, weed pressure, and overall condition of the turf, but the goal stays the same: cleaner, more controlled outdoor spaces.

Best For

Properties dealing with visible weed pressure in the rocks, along edges, or throughout the yard.

Service Approach

Price varies by total square feet of treatment. Treatments are done in spring (Mar/Apr) and late summer (Aug/Sep).
